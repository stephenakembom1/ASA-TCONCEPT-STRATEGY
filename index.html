ASA TCONCEPT Trading Models

These six advanced models form the foundation of the ASA TCONCEPT strategy. Each one is built on real market behavior and can be used across all Forex pairs.

Model 1: High Sweeps Reversal model

This ASA TCONCEPT Illustrate a price movement where the price trades up, sweeps the high of the previous candle and then moves downward towards the previous low

Model 2: Low Sweeps Reversal Model

This ASA TCONCEPT Illustrate a price movement where the price trades down, sweeps the low of the previous candle and then moves upward towards the previous high.

Model 3: Failed Low Retracement Model

This ASA TCONCEPT Model represent a price movement where the price retrace to the 50%, 62%, 79% fibonacci levels, fails to sweep the previous low and then moves upward.

Model 4: Failed High Retracement Model

This ASA TCONCEPT Model represent a price movement where the price retrace to the 50%, 62%, 79% fibonacci levels, fails to sweep the previous high and then moves downward.

Model 5: Break and Retest Continuation Model upward

This ASA TCONCEPT Illustrate a price movement where the price trades upward, breaks the previous high then retest it before Continuing further upward.

Model 6: Break and Retest Continuation Model downward

This ASA TCONCEPT Illustrate a price movement where the price trades downward, breaks the previous low then retest it before Continuing further downward.
